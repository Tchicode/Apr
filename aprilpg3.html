Celebrations and Observances in April

Buddha's Birthday

Buddha's birthday is said to be in the month of April. He was born in Nepal and his birthday is referred to as Purnima. Purnima is the first full moon and is a time to celebrate Gurus and Teachers. Buddha is strongly associated with enlightenment.

This is a Golden Statue of Buddha

Easter

Another major celebration, that is often in April, is Easter. Easter is commercially associated with colorful eggs and bunny rabbits. What easter really represents is the sacrifice and resurrection of Jesus Christ. Christ is associated with forgiveness, love and rebirth.

A Dipiction of Jesus Christ Ascending

Passover

Still, another major celebration often in April is Passover. What is it about April? Passover commemorates the ending of bondage of enslaved Israelites, under the protection and guidance of a man named Moses. Moses was born of a enslaved mother, but raised as royalty. Passover is often associated with being freed and having help in high places.

Here is Moses Leading the Israelites Out of Slavery
